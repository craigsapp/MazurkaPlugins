MzFindSilence          

SYNOPSIS

    MzFindSilence -- Marks regions of silence in an audio file.

OUTPUTS
    MzFindSilence generates 2 outputs:

    1.
    2. Always Contains only two markers
      (1) the start of the audio data after any initial silence. (2) the end of the audio data before any final silence.
